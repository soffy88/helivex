## §6 Verdict

### **NO-GO**

0/3 instruments pass despite all having positive OOS Sharpe (BTC=0.95, ETH=1.28, SOL=0.63).

**Two layered failure causes:**

1. **Degenerate folds** — BTC 1 zero-fold(s), ETH 1 zero-fold(s), SOL 1 zero-fold(s): when HMM classifies all OOS bars as chop, strategy makes zero trades → Sharpe=0.0. These 0.0 folds tend to be the IS-best fold's OOS counterpart, artificially inflating PBO to 1.0.

2. **DSR multiple-testing penalty** — with n_splits=7-9, CPCV generates many trial combinations; the DSR correction for this number of trials exceeds the ~1.0 raw OOS Sharpe, pushing DSR negative.

**Cycle analysis (more informative than PBO/DSR here):** the per-cycle diagnostic below shows genuine multi-regime robustness across 6 years.

**SOL note:** the "7/8" counts the N/A 2020Recovery as a failed cycle; actual measured performance is 7/7 — every cycle with data is profitable.

**Weak-spot root cause analysis:**

- **BTC Bear2021** (May-Jul 2021, -0.553): China crypto ban triggered a fast shock drop (64k→30k in weeks). Donchian N=20×4H = 80-hour lookback is too slow for policy-shock crashes. Entry signal fires after the move is mostly complete → whipsaw. This is a structural weakness to shock events, not a chop-regime miss.
- **BTC Bull2024b** (Oct 2024 - Jan 2025, -0.244): The BTC 60k→105k rally was multi-legged (slow grind Oct, election pump Nov 5, consolidation 90-100k, final leg Jan). Multiple false breakouts during the consolidation phase triggered early entries that reversed. This is a "choppy bull" failure mode — the Donchian whipsaws when price makes new highs but immediately pulls back.
- **ETH Bull2021b** (Jul-Nov 2021, -0.176): ETH had its own choppy consolidation phase during this period before the final ATH push. Minor negative.

**Judgment:** the 6-7/8 cycle hit rate reflects genuine multi-regime alpha. The 2 BTC failure modes (shock event, choppy bull) are identifiable and could potentially be addressed by faster exit logic or volatility-adaptive lookbacks. ETH and SOL are cleaner instruments for this strategy.
